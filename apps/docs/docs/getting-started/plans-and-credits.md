# Plans and Pricing

Shogo uses a usage-based pricing model. You pay for AI usage in USD, based on the raw provider costs (from OpenAI, Anthropic, etc.) plus a 20% markup.

## How pricing works

- Pricing is **per token** based on the AI model used, not a flat rate per message. Input and output tokens are priced separately to accurately reflect model costs.
- We charge the provider's raw cost + 20% (e.g., if Claude costs $0.003/1K tokens, you pay $0.0036).
- The chat input shows an estimated USD cost before you send.
- Daily and monthly allowances reset automatically. Overage charges (when enabled) are billed on top of your plan.
- You can check your remaining balance in the sidebar or in **Settings > Billing**.

:::tip
You don't use allowance when browsing the dashboard, viewing the canvas, or navigating your workspace. Costs are only incurred when the AI processes a message.
:::

## Available plans

- Create unlimited agents
- Access to all templates
- Perfect for exploring Shogo and building your first agents

## Managing your subscription

1. Go to **Settings** in the sidebar and open the **Billing** tab.
2. Here you can:
   - View your current plan and remaining balance
   - See your usage history (both included and overage)
   - Upgrade or downgrade your plan
   - Manage your payment method via Stripe
   - Enable overage and set a hard cap to control spending

## Frequently asked questions

**What happens when I run out of allowance?**
By default, you won't be able to send messages to the AI until your allowance resets or you upgrade. If you enable overage and have a payment method on file, you'll be charged for usage beyond your allowance.

**What's the 20% markup?**
Shogo charges raw provider costs (OpenAI, Anthropic, etc.) plus a 20% markup to cover infrastructure, support, and development.

**Can I set a spending limit?**
Yes. On the Billing page, you can enable overage and optionally set a hard cap. Once hit, no charges will incur until the next billing period.

**Do unused allowances roll over?**
No, your daily and monthly allowances reset at the start of each period. Overage charges are billed separately and don't roll over.

**Can I downgrade my plan?**
Yes, you can downgrade at any time. The change takes effect at the start of your next billing period.

**Is there a free trial for paid plans?**
The free plan lets you explore Shogo at no cost ($0.50 daily). When you're ready for more allowance, upgrade to a Pro or Business plan.
