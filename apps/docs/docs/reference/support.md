## Ask the AI

The AI agent in your project can often help resolve issues:

> "Something isn't working with [feature]. Can you investigate and fix it?"

> "I'm getting an error when I [action]. Can you help?"

The AI can diagnose and fix many issues faster than external support.

## Contact support

If you need human assistance:

- **Email**: Contact us through the **Help** menu in the app
- **In-app help**: Click the help icon in the sidebar

### When contacting support, include:

1. **What you were trying to do** — Describe the task or feature you were working on
2. **What happened** — Describe the unexpected behavior
3. **What you expected** — Describe what should have happened
4. **Screenshots** — If applicable, include screenshots showing the issue
5. **Project details** — The project name and workspace

## Response times

Response times depend on the nature of the issue and your plan:

| Priority | Description | Target response |
|----------|-------------|-----------------|
| Critical | Published app is down or data loss | Within hours |
| High | Feature not working, blocking your workflow | Within 1 business day |
| Medium | Non-urgent issue, question, or suggestion | Within 2 business days |
| Low | Feature request, general inquiry | Within 5 business days |

## Feature requests

Have an idea for improving Shogo? We'd love to hear it:

1. Use the **Feedback** option in the Help menu.
2. Describe the feature you'd like and why it would be useful.
3. Include examples or screenshots if they help illustrate your idea.

## Bug reports

If you've found a bug:

1. Note the steps to reproduce the issue.
2. Take a screenshot or record the behavior.
3. Report it through the **Help** menu with as much detail as possible.

For information about Shogo's service status and any ongoing issues, check the status page linked in the app footer.
