# Workspaces & Members

Workspaces are how you organize your projects and collaborate with others. Each workspace has its own projects, members, and billing.

## What is a workspace?

A workspace is a shared space for your projects. Think of it like a team folder:

- All your projects live inside a workspace
- You can invite team members to collaborate
- Billing and credits are managed at the workspace level
- You can belong to multiple workspaces (e.g., one for your company, one for personal projects)

## Creating a workspace

When you sign up for Shogo, you'll create your first workspace. To create additional workspaces:

1. Click your workspace name in the sidebar.
2. Select **Create Workspace**.
3. Enter a name for your workspace.
4. Click **Create**.

## Switching between workspaces

If you belong to multiple workspaces:

1. Click your workspace name in the sidebar.
2. Select the workspace you want to switch to.

Your dashboard will update to show that workspace's projects.

## Inviting members

To add someone to your workspace:

1. Go to **Members** in the sidebar.
2. Click **Invite Member**.
3. Enter their email address.
4. Choose their role:
   - **Owner** — Full access, including billing and workspace settings
   - **Member** — Can create, edit, and view projects
5. Click **Send Invitation**.

The person will receive an email invitation. Once they accept, they'll appear in your members list.

:::info Pending invitations
You can see pending invitations in the Members page. Invitations that haven't been accepted yet will show as "Pending."
:::

## Managing members

From the **Members** page, you can:

- **View all members** — See who has access to your workspace
- **Change roles** — Promote a member to owner or change an owner to member
- **Remove members** — Remove someone from the workspace

## Workspace settings

Go to **Settings** in the sidebar to manage:

- **Workspace name** — Rename your workspace
- **Billing** — View and manage your plan and credits
- **Members** — Manage who has access

## How billing works with workspaces

Credits and subscriptions are tied to the workspace, not to individual users. This means:

- All members of a workspace share the workspace's credit pool
- The workspace owner manages the billing plan
- Upgrading the plan gives more credits to the entire workspace

See [Plans and Credits](../getting-started/plans-and-credits) for pricing details.

## FAQ

**Can I be in multiple workspaces?**
Yes. You can belong to as many workspaces as you like. Each has its own projects, members, and billing.

**Can I move a project between workspaces?**
Currently, projects belong to the workspace they were created in.

**What happens if I leave a workspace?**
You'll lose access to that workspace's projects. Your personal data and other workspaces are not affected.

**Who pays for the workspace?**
The workspace owner manages billing. All members benefit from the workspace's credit pool.
