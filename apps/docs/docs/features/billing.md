# Billing & Subscriptions

Manage your workspace's plan, view credit usage, and handle payment information from the Billing page.

## Accessing billing

1. Go to **Settings** in the sidebar.
2. Click **Billing**.

Only workspace **owners** can manage billing settings.

## What you'll see

### Current plan

Your active plan is displayed at the top, showing:

- Plan name and tier (e.g., Pro 500)
- Credits included per month
- Monthly or annual billing cycle

### Credit balance

A summary of your credit usage:

- **Credits remaining** — How many credits you have left this period
- **Credits used** — How many you've used so far
- **Reset date** — When your credits reset

### Usage history

A log of credit usage, showing when credits were spent and on which projects.

## Upgrading your plan

To get more credits:

1. Go to **Billing**.
2. Click **Upgrade** or **Change Plan**.
3. Select your new plan and tier.
4. Confirm your selection.

Upgrades take effect immediately. You'll receive the additional credits right away.

## Downgrading your plan

1. Go to **Billing**.
2. Click **Change Plan**.
3. Select a lower tier.
4. Confirm your selection.

Downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing period. You keep your current credits until then.

## Payment management

Billing is handled through **Stripe**, a secure payment platform. From the billing page, you can:

- **Update payment method** — Change your credit card or payment source
- **View invoices** — See past charges and download receipts
- **Cancel subscription** — End your paid plan (reverts to Free at period end)

## Billing FAQ

**Who manages billing for a workspace?**
Workspace owners manage billing. Members can view their credit balance but cannot change the plan.

**What happens if my payment fails?**
If a payment fails, you'll receive a notification. Your plan remains active for a grace period while you update your payment method. If the issue isn't resolved, your workspace will revert to the Free plan.

**Can I get a refund?**
Contact support for refund requests. Refund eligibility depends on the circumstances.

**Do I get charged when I publish?**
No. Publishing is included with your plan at no extra cost. You only spend credits when sending messages to the AI.

**Can different workspace members be on different plans?**
No. Plans are workspace-level. All members share the workspace's credit pool.

**How do I cancel my subscription?**
Go to **Billing** and click the subscription management link. You can cancel at any time. Your plan remains active until the end of the current billing period.
