# Live Preview

The live preview shows your running app in real time, right next to the chat. As you send messages to the AI and changes are made, the preview updates instantly so you can see exactly what your app will look like.

## How it works

The preview runs a live version of your app inside the project editor. It's not a static mockup — it's the actual app, fully interactive. You can click buttons, fill in forms, navigate between pages, and test functionality.

When the AI makes changes to your app (adding a page, modifying a component, changing styles), the preview refreshes automatically to show the updated version.

## Preview controls

### Viewport sizes

Use the viewport buttons at the top of the preview to switch between:

- **Desktop** — Full-width view, as it would appear on a laptop or desktop screen
- **Tablet** — Medium-width view, simulating a tablet device
- **Mobile** — Narrow view, simulating a phone screen

This helps you check that your app looks good on all devices.

### URL navigation

If your app has multiple pages (like a Dashboard, Settings, and Profile), you can navigate between them using:

- The **URL bar** at the top of the preview — type a route like `/settings` to jump directly to a page
- **Links and navigation** inside your app — click links and buttons just like a real user would

### Refresh

Click the **Refresh** button to reload the preview. This is useful if:

- The preview seems stuck or out of date
- You want to reset the app state (like clearing form inputs)
- You want to see the app from its initial state

## Tips

:::tip Test as you build
After each AI change, interact with the preview to make sure things work as expected. It's easier to fix issues one at a time than to discover them all at the end.
:::

:::tip Check all viewport sizes
If your app will be used on phones or tablets, switch between viewport sizes periodically to catch layout issues early.
:::

## FAQ

**Why isn't the preview updating?**
The preview updates automatically after each AI change. If it seems stuck, try clicking the Refresh button. If the issue persists, check the chat for any error messages from the AI.

**Can I interact with the preview?**
Yes! The preview is fully interactive. Click buttons, fill in forms, navigate pages — everything works as it would for a real user.

**Does the preview cost credits?**
No. Only messages sent to the AI cost credits. Viewing and interacting with the preview is free.
