# Affiliate Program

Earn recurring commissions by referring new customers to Shogo. The program is opt-in: nothing changes for your account until you explicitly enroll and get your unique referral link.

## How it works

1. **Enroll** — Opt in from the Affiliate section of the app to claim your unique referral link.
2. **Share your link** — Send it to anyone who might benefit from Shogo. The link sets a referral cookie that lasts 60 days.
3. **They sign up and pay** — When someone you referred starts a paid Shogo subscription, you're credited as their referrer.
4. **You earn** — Commissions accrue on each of their paid invoices and are paid out to your connected bank account.

## Commission rate

You earn **20% of each referred customer's seat subscription for the first 12 months**, then **10% forever after** for as long as they remain a paying customer.

- Commissions are calculated on the **seat subscription** (per-seat plan revenue).
- **Usage overage is excluded** from the commission basis. Metered usage beyond a plan's window is billed at the provider's raw cost plus markup and represents infrastructure passthrough, not plan revenue, so it does not earn commission.

## Payouts

- Commissions are held during a **refund window** before they become payable, so refunded or charged-back invoices don't pay out.
- Once eligible, balances are paid to a **Stripe-connected bank account**. You'll be prompted to connect one before your first payout.
- Payouts are batched and only sent once your approved balance clears a minimum threshold.
- Stripe issues a **1099-NEC** if you earn $600 or more in a calendar year (US affiliates).

## Eligibility and rules

- **Self-referrals are not eligible** — you can't use your own link to earn on your own subscription.
- Customers who subscribe through **Apple in-app purchase** are excluded from earning commissions.
- Commissions may be **reversed on refund or chargeback**.
- When sharing your link, you must follow the **FTC disclosure requirements** for affiliate relationships.
