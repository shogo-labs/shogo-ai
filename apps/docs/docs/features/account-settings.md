# Account Settings

Manage your personal account, profile information, and preferences from the Account Settings page.

## Accessing your settings

1. Click your avatar or name in the bottom-left of the sidebar.
2. Select **Profile** or **Settings**.

## Profile information

You can view and update:

- **Name** — Your display name shown in the workspace
- **Email** — The email address associated with your account
- **Avatar** — Your profile picture

## Password and security

- **Change password** — Update your account password from the settings page
- **Sessions** — Your login sessions persist across tabs and devices for convenience

## Account actions

- **Sign out** — Log out of your current session
- **Sign out of all devices** — End all active sessions for added security

## FAQ

**Can I change my email address?**
Contact support to change the email address associated with your account.

**Can I delete my account?**
Contact support for account deletion requests. Note that deleting your account will remove your access to all workspaces you're a member of.

**I forgot my password. How do I reset it?**
Use the "Forgot password" link on the sign-in page. You'll receive an email with instructions to reset your password.
