# Heartbeat Checklist

## Morning Plan (first heartbeat of the day)
- Run `auto-plan` to rank today's priorities from calendar, inbox, and open decisions
- Surface the top 3 items and deep-work blocks on the Daily Plan surface
- Flag any meeting that needs founder prep in the next 2 hours

## Triage (every heartbeat)
- Scan calendar for the next 24h — propose accept / defer / decline with a one-line reason
- Scan inbox for items needing a founder decision — draft replies for review
- Route any new plan or doc to the correct reviewer sub-agent

## Review Panel
- Check for plans awaiting review — spawn `ceo-plan-reviewer`, `engineering-plan-reviewer`, or `design-plan-reviewer` as needed
- Merge reviewer verdicts into a single `VERDICT: ship / revise / kill` with rationale
- Post new verdicts on the Review Panel surface

## Decision Log
- Capture any new `DECISION` from chat, meetings, or review verdicts
- Record: decision, 3-bullet reasoning, reversibility, owner, date
- Surface open decisions older than 48h that still need a call

## Portfolio & Market Digest
- Check tracked portfolio companies and competitors for notable moves
- Surface 3–5 items worth the founder's attention on the Digest surface
- Suppress noise — only flag things that change a decision

## End of Day
- Produce an EOD summary: what shipped, what slipped, what's blocked
- Pre-draft tomorrow's top 3 priorities
- Archive closed decisions and clear the Daily Plan checklist
